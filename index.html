About Us

FOOTBALL AGENT FOR THE TALENTED(FAFTT) is a passion driven sports agency which recruit, equip and connect young talents in the field of sports and exposes them to challenging scope of success.

By providing facilities not only for young to middle aged adults and older adults, but children below high school age. Our company provides services to people who are still passionate about their sports but have been systemically isolated from them.

PARTNERSHIP

TALENT EXPO ENTERTAINMENT (TEE) is an event management service and sports event organizer that is located in Belgium that has interest in supporting African education, companies and individuals recognizing, appreciating and rewarding them for their great impact in Africa and beyond

Talent Expo Entertainment (TEE) aim is raising entrepreneurs, creating and developing platforms for talents showcase their creativity with the aim of reducing unemployment and dependency ratio of youths in the society. We are also in partnership with various International Organizations enabling us to achieve our vision, creating new business opportunities, building strong international relationships and networks as well and soliciting grants in aid of developmental projexts to ensure the smooth, effective, and efficient management of less privileged people within Africa.
